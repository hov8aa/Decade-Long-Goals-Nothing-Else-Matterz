# Privacy policy — Decade Long Goals

**Last updated:** July 2026

Decade Long Goals doesn't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Here's exactly how the app works:

## What the app stores

Everything you enter — your decade-long goal, its history of changes, your daily 10-minute sessions — is saved **only on your device**, using Android's local app storage. None of it is sent anywhere.

## What the app does *not* do

- No account, no sign-up, no login
- No internet connection required to use the app
- No analytics, tracking, or crash reporting
- No advertising, no third-party SDKs
- No data shared with us, or with anyone else, because none is collected

## Deleting your data

Uninstalling the app removes all of its stored data from your device. There is nothing held anywhere else to delete.

## Changes to this policy

If this ever changes — for example, if a future version adds an optional backup feature — this page will be updated first, and the change will describe exactly what's new and why.
